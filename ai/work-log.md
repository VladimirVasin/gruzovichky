- 2026-04-08: Fixed driver walk stuck bug. `UpdateDriverWalk` was computing arrival using full 3D `sqrMagnitude`, but the driver only moves along XZ. When a waypoint's Y differs from the driver Y (terrain height variation), the XZ target is reached but the Y delta keeps the threshold unsatisfied → driver freezes indefinitely. Fixed by zeroing Y before the arrival check (`flatDelta.y = 0f`). File: `GameBootstrap.Transport.cs`.
